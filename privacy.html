Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use TikTok_tool, we may collect the following information through the TikTok API with your explicit authorization:

  • Profile Information: Your TikTok display name, username, profile picture, bio, and verification status
  • Statistics: Follower count, following count, likes count, and video count
  • Video Data: Your public video list and related metadata

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Provide and maintain the Service
  • Display your TikTok profile information within the app
  • Enable content sharing and posting features
  • Improve the user experience

3. Data Storage

We do not permanently store your TikTok data on our servers. Data is accessed in real-time through the TikTok API and is only cached temporarily during your active session.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. Your TikTok data is only used within the Service as described in this policy.

5. TikTok API

Our use of TikTok data is governed by TikTok's API Terms of Service and Developer Policies. We comply with all TikTok data handling requirements.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Revoke access to your TikTok data at any time through your TikTok account settings
  • Request deletion of any cached data
  • Request information about what data we access

7. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

8. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
