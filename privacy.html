Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use TikTok_tool, we may collect the following information through the TikTok API with your explicit authorization:

  • Profile Information: Your TikTok display name, username, profile picture, bio, and verification status
  • Statistics: Follower count, following count, likes count, and video count
  • Video Data: Your public video list and related metadata

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Provide and maintain the Service
  • Display your TikTok profile information within the app
  • Enable content sharing and posting features
  • Improve the user experience

3. Data Storage and Retention

We do not permanently store your TikTok data on our servers. Data is accessed in real-time through the TikTok API and is only cached temporarily during your active browser session. All cached data is automatically deleted when your session ends or when you log out. We do not retain any personal data beyond your active session.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

TikTok_tool uses browser sessionStorage to maintain your login state during an active session. We do not use cookies, third-party analytics, advertising trackers, or any other persistent tracking technologies. No data is stored beyond the duration of your browser session.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. Your TikTok data is only used within the Service as described in this policy.

6. TikTok API

Our use of TikTok data is governed by TikTok's API Terms of Service and Developer Policies. We comply with all TikTok data handling requirements.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at marketing@blinx.jp.

The Service is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly.
